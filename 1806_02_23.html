Thomas Beddoes to Davies Giddy, 23 February 1806

Dear Giddy

I perceive that these American stocks give you a great deal of trouble but I hope a great part of that trouble is taken at the same time on yr own acct –

The stock giving 52/3 interest with its other advantages seems to me quite as eligible as any other – if you think so, I wd have you act accordingly –

This is the 3rd letter from me which one post will bring you – one of the others will contain the letter of atty I hope duly executed –

I am Dear Giddy

Thomas Beddoes
